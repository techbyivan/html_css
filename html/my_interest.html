Ok, First Things First...

  • Who invented it...

    • Although this can be a contentious topic for advanced engineers, one of the first documents referencing the topic & theory, was
    by a gentlemen named Donald Hebb in 1949. He wrote a book entitled The Organization of Behavior.

  • What is it...

    • Machine learning is the study of computer algorithms that improve automatically through experience.

  • When is it used...

    • Machine Learning personalization algorithms are used to offer product recommendations to customers, amongst an assortment
    of other things.

  • Where can I find it...

    • You can find it in many software applications from U.S. Government internal systems to your favorite ride sharing mobile apps such as
    Uber & Lyft.

  • Why is it important...

    • It's important because technology is moving at such a rapid pace, it is our responsibility to acknowledge and recognize
    the power of algorithms. These algorithms will shape and influence our existence as humans.
